How to play

The basics

  • Each round, pick team(s) you believe will win and advance.
  • If any of your picks lose, you are eliminated from the league.
  • You can only pick each team once across the entire tournament — choose wisely!
  • All picks must be submitted before the first game of each round begins.
  • If you miss the deadline, you are automatically eliminated.
  • Picks are hidden until the round's deadline passes.

Strategy tip

  • Since you can't reuse a team, save the strongest favorites (Brazil, France, Argentina, England, Spain) for later rounds when you need them most.
  • Early rounds are a good time to use solid but less elite teams — teams likely to win one match but not go all the way.
